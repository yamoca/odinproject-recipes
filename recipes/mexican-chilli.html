Mexican Sausage and Bean Chilli

Description

Say adios to boring with this Mexican sausage and bean chilli. It's sure to spice up your mealtimes. Easy to make, full of flavour, and delightfully exotic, this dish is certainly a crowd pleaser. So, grab a pan and get your fiesta on!

Ingredients

  • meat free sausages (4)
  • Napolina five bean salad (tinned, 400g)
  • chopped tomatoes (tinned, 400g)
  • tomato paste (1 tbsp)
  • diced small leak (1)
  • diced pepper (1)
  • vegetable stock (200ml)
  • cloved garlic (2)
  • red chillies (2)
  • ground cumin (2 tsp)
  • oregano (2 tsp)
  • smoked paprika (2 tsp)
  • ground coriander (2tsp)
  • fresh coriander (small bunch)

Method

  1. Place the Quorn Sausages into a pre-heated oven as per packaging instructions.
  2. Lightly fry the red onion, leek, garlic and chilli.
  3. Add the spices, and continue to fry. You may need to add a splash of water if it becomes too dry.
  4. Add the diced pepper, then the vegetable stock.
  5. Add the tinned tomatoes and bring to the boul, then leave to simmer.
  6. Chop the Quorn sausages into chunks and throw in along with the beans.
  7. Leave to cook for approx. 20 minutes, and then finish with chopped coriander.
  8. Serve with basmati rice and a wedge of lime.
